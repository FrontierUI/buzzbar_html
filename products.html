Berry blast

A fusion of mixed berries, Berry Blast packs a punch with its combination of blueberries, raspberries, and strawberries. Each puff delivers a medley of flavors, with the sweet and tart profile of raspberries leading the charge, complemented by the juicy sweetness of strawberries and the mild tang of blueberries. The layered complexity of this flavor keeps it exciting, with a smooth vapor that fills your senses with a refreshing berry experience that lingers long after the exhale.

cherry crush

Cherry Crush delivers a powerful burst of sweet and tart cherries, mimicking the taste of perfectly ripened fruit. It starts with a bold, juicy flavor that lingers on the tongue, followed by a slightly sour undertone that gives the vape a balanced complexity. The smooth vapor makes it ideal for both all-day use and special occasions, leaving a refreshing aftertaste with every inhale. It's a must-try for fruit lovers and those who enjoy a vibrant vaping experience with a classic twist.

Banana bliss

Indulge in the creamy richness of Banana Bliss, a smooth and satisfying flavor that captures the essence of ripe, sweet bananas with a velvety texture. The inhale introduces a burst of banana goodness, reminiscent of fresh banana slices, while the exhale delivers a subtle hint of vanilla and cream for a balanced, dessert-like finish. This flavor is perfect for those who enjoy fruity vapes with a creamy undertone, offering a mellow yet flavorful experience that's ideal for both everyday vaping and a relaxing end to the day.

Blueberry burst

Blueberry Burst is a vibrant flavor that brings the juicy essence of fresh blueberries to every puff. The vape opens with a slightly tart, natural blueberry flavor that hits your taste buds with a burst of fruitiness. As you exhale, the flavor mellows into a sweeter, more rounded berry finish, leaving a lasting sensation of summer freshness. This is an ideal option for fruit enthusiasts who want a bold, yet balanced, vape experience with a lingering sweetness.

Mint fusion

For those seeking a cool, refreshing vape, Mint Fusion offers an icy blend of crisp peppermint and smooth menthol. The flavor starts with a strong hit of mint that awakens the senses, while the menthol finish adds a cooling, soothing effect, perfect for hot days or when you need to unwind. This flavor provides a clean and invigorating experience with a long-lasting chill that is great for those who enjoy menthol-based or refreshing vapes.
